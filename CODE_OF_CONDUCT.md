# Code of Conduct

DocWise follows a contributor covenant based on respect, professionalism, and
inclusive collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, merge requests, reviews, and chat.
- Assume good intent while asking clear questions.
- Give actionable feedback focused on the work.
- Respect privacy when discussing documents, receipts, reports, or logs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or abusive language.
- Publishing private information, secrets, or sensitive document contents.
- Deliberately disrupting reviews, CI, or project operations.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove harmful content, close discussions, request changes in
behavior, or restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and
its contributors.
